Cheese omelette

Servings:
1
Cooking time:
5 min
Difficulty:

Description

Rustle up a simple omelette for a quick and easy snack any time of day. Use mature cheddar for maximum cheesy flavour

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 15g mature cheddar, finely grated

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a jug and whisk well with a fork. Season with a pinch of salt.
  2. Warm oil and butter in a medium non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat until the butter starts to foam.
  3. Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to cover the base. Use a spatula to gently draw in the eggs from four points to create folds in the center.
  4. Let the eggs cook gently for 2-3 minutes until slightly undercooked in the center.
  5. Sprinkle cheese over the eggs and gently fold the omelette in half using the spatula.
  6. Turn off the heat and let the omelette sit for 1 minute to melt the cheese. Serve with a sprinkle of black pepper.
