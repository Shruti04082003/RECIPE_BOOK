Ultimate chocolate cake

Servings:
1
Cooking time:
1 hr - 1 hr 30 min
Difficulty:

Description

Indulge yourself with this ultimate chocolate ganache cake recipe that is beautifully moist, rich and fudgy. Perfect for a celebration or an afternoon tea

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (about 60% cocoa solids), chopped
  • 200g butter, cubed
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee granules
  • 85g self-raising flour
  • 85g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 200g light muscovado sugar
  • 200g golden caster sugar
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 75ml buttermilk
  • 50g grated chocolate or 100g curls, to decorate

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 160C/fan 140C/gas 3. Butter and line a 20cm round cake tin.
  2. Melt 200g chopped dark chocolate and 200g butter in a medium pan.
  3. Dissolve 1 tbsp instant coffee granules in 125ml cold water and add to the pan.
  4. Warm over low heat until melted, or microwave for about 5 minutes, stirring halfway.
  5. Mix 85g self-raising flour, 85g plain flour, ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda, 200g light muscovado sugar, 200g golden caster sugar, and 25g cocoa powder.
  6. Beat 3 medium eggs with 75ml buttermilk.
  7. Combine the melted chocolate mixture, egg mixture, and flour mixture until smooth and runny.
  8. Pour into the tin and bake for 1hr 25-1hr 30 mins. A skewer should come out clean from the center.
  9. Cool in the tin, then turn out onto a wire rack. Once cold, cut the cake horizontally into three layers.
  10. For the ganache, heat 300ml double cream with 2 tbsp golden caster sugar until almost boiling. Pour over 200g chopped dark chocolate and stir until smooth.
  11. Cool the ganache until slightly thicker but still pourable. Sandwich the cake layers with a bit of ganache, then pour the rest over the cake, smoothing with a palette knife.
  12. Decorate with 50g grated chocolate or 100g chocolate curls. The cake stays moist for 3-4 days.
